Measurements:

Bit: Zero or One (on/off)
Byte: 8 bits
Kilobyte (KB): 1024 bits
Megabyte (MB): 1024 Kilobytes
Gigabyte (GB): 1024 Megabytes
etc...

Generally one character is 8 bits which is 1 byte (This depends on the standard, eg: ASCII or UTF-8)

Binary go up in powers of 2 as there are two options, zero or one.
256 different characters can be stored using a single byte.

  8|  7|  6|  5|  4|  3|  2|  1|
---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
128| 64| 32| 16|  8|  4|  2|  1|

0001 = 1
0010 = 2
0011 = 3
0100 = 4
etc...

|Decimal|  Binary   | Character|
--------------------------------

|    65 | 0100 0001 |        A |
|    97 | 0110 0001 |        a |
|    51 | 0011 0011 |        3 |
